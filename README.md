## 🧠 What is NexiriAI?

**NexiriAI** is a real-time crypto intelligence and trading dashboard powered by AI insights. It combines live market data with AI-generated analysis to help users make smarter, faster decisions in the crypto market.

Rather than just showing charts, NexiriAI explains what's happening — surfacing trends, anomalies, and actionable signals through an integrated AI layer powered by the **Gemini Flash API**.

---

## ✨ Planned Features

### 📊 Market Intelligence
- **Market Cap Overview** — Real-time global crypto market cap, dominance charts, and 24h volume tracking
- **Gainers & Losers** — Top performing and worst performing coins with interactive filtering
- **Coin Analytics** — Deep-dive per-coin view with price history, volume trends, and market sentiment

### 🤖 AI Layer (Gemini Flash API)
- **AI Market Summary** — Natural language daily briefings on overall market conditions
- **Coin Insights** — Ask questions about any coin and get context-aware AI analysis
- **Trend Detection** — AI-flagged anomalies, breakout patterns, and sentiment shifts

### 📈 Interactive Data Visualizations (ECharts)
- Multi-timeframe candlestick and line charts
- Market dominance pie/donut charts
- Volume heatmaps across coins and timeframes
- Gainers/losers bar race animations

### 🔐 User Experience
- Dark/light mode with persistent preference
- Fully responsive across desktop and mobile
- Watchlist with custom coin tracking
- Real-time data refresh with loading state feedback
